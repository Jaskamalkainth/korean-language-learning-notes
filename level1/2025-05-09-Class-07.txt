물건 사기 1 (Shopping 1) [mulgeon sagi]

Dialogue 1:
A: 민수 씨, 어디 가요? [minsu ssi, eodi gayo?] → Where are you going, Minsoo?
B: 시장에 가요. [sijange gayo] → I'm going to the market.
A: 무엇을 사요? [mueoseul sayo?] → What will you buy?
B: 사과를 사요. [sagwareul sayo] → I will buy some apples.

Dialogue 2:
점원: 무엇을 드릴까요? [mueoseul deurilkkayo?] → May I help you?
손님: 사과는 얼마예요? [sagwaneun eolmayeyo?] → How much are the apples?
점원: 1,000원이에요. [cheon won-ieyo] → They are 1,000 won.
손님: 사과를 주세요. [sagwareul juseyo] → I'll take the apples, please.

Grammar Points (문법 - munbeop):

1. 을/를 (Object Marker)
Used after a noun to show it is the object of the sentence.
Examples:
사과를 사요 [sagwareul sayo] — Buy an apple
책을 읽어요 [chaegeul ilgeoyo] — Read a book

2. -(으)세요 (Honorific request)
Attached to verbs to politely ask or request:
Examples:
사과를 주세요 [sagwareul juseyo] — Please give me apples
책을 읽으세요 [chaegeul ilgeuseyo] — Please read a book
한국어를 공부하세요 [hangugeoreul gongbuhaseyo] — Please study Korean

Practical Exercises (실습):

1. Role-play Shopping Situations
Example:
A: 펜은 얼마예요? [peneun eolmayeyo?] — How much is the pen?
B: 700원이에요. [chilbaek won-ieyo] — It's 700 won.
A: 펜을 주세요. [peneul juseyo] — Please give me the pen.

2. Receipt Reading & Price Questions
Practice reading a receipt and answering:
물은 얼마예요? [mureun eolmayeyo?] — 500원 [obaek won]
빵은 얼마예요? [ppangeun eolmayeyo?] — 1,300원 [cheon sambaek won]

3. Buying Dialogue Completion
Example:
A: 무엇을 사요? [mueoseul sayo?] — What will you buy?
B: 우유를 사요. [uyureul sayo] — I will buy milk.
A: 무엇을 먹어요? [mueoseul meogeoyo?] — What will you eat?
B: 빵을 먹어요. [ppangeul meogeoyo] — I will eat bread.

4. Memo Practice
Write or read a memo about buying items:
"사과가 600원이에요. 오늘 시장에 가요."
[sagwaga yukbaek won-ieyo. oneul sijange gayo.]
— "Apples are 600 won. I'm going to the market today."

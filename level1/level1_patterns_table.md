# Sejong Korean 1 — Grammar Patterns Quick Reference Table

> All patterns from Units 1–10 with a Korean example, romanization, English translation, and grammar meaning.

| Pattern | Korean Example | Romanization | English Translation | Meaning |
|---------|---------------|--------------|---------------------|---------|
| 이에요/예요 | 저는 학생이에요 | jeoneun haksaengieyo | I am a student. | is / am / are |
| 은/는 | 민수 씨는 한국 사람이에요 | Minsu ssineun hanguk saramieyo | Minsoo is Korean. | topic particle |
| ~아/어요 | 지금 공부해요 | jigeum gongbuhaeyo | I'm studying now. | present tense |
| 에 가다 | 학교에 가요 | hakgyoe gayo | I go to school. | go to (place) |
| 이/가 | 가방이 있어요 | gabangi isseoyo | There is a bag. | subject particle |
| 에 있다/없다 | 책상 위에 있어요 | chaeksang wie isseoyo | It's on the desk. | exists at (place) |
| 을/를 | 우유를 사요 | uyureul sayo | I buy milk. | object particle |
| (으)세요 | 책을 읽으세요 | chaegeul ilgeuseyo | Please read the book. | please do (polite request) |
| -ㅂ니다/습니다 | 저는 유키입니다 | jeoneun Yukiipnida | I am Yuki. | formal polite statement |
| -ㅂ니까/습니까 | 무엇을 삽니까? | mueoseul samnikka | What will you buy? | formal polite question |
| 하고 | 사과하고 빵을 사요 | sagwahago ppangeul sayo | I buy apples and bread. | and / with (nouns) |
| ~았/었어요 | 어제 영화를 봤어요 | eoje yeonghwareul bwasseoyo | I watched a movie yesterday. | past tense |
| 에서 | 카페에서 공부해요 | kapeseo gongbuhaeyo | I study at the café. | at / in (action location) |
| 그리고 | 춥고. 그리고 눈이 와요 | chupgo. geurigo nuni wayo | It's cold. And it's snowing. | and / in addition |
| 안 | 오늘은 안 더워요 | oneureun an deowoyo | It's not hot today. | not / don't (negation) |
| 에 (time) | 월요일에 가요 | woryoire gayo | I go on Monday. | at / on / in (time) |
| O시 O분 | 세 시 삼십 분이에요 | se si samsip bunieyo | It is 3:30. | telling time |
| ~(으)ㄹ까요 | 같이 갈까요? | gachi galkayo | Shall we go together? | shall we? / suggestion |
| ~아/어요 (suggestion) | 같이 먹어요 | gachi meogeoyo | Let's eat together. | let's do (suggestion) |
| 그래서 | 바빠요. 그래서 못 가요 | bappayo. geuraeseo mot gayo | I'm busy. So I can't go. | so / therefore |
| ~(으)ㄹ 거예요 | 주말에 여행할 거예요 | jumare yeohaenghal geoyeyo | I'm going to travel this weekend. | will / going to (future) |

| Number | Korean | Reading |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 1 | 하나 | hana |
| 2 | 둘 | dul |
| 3 | 셋 | set |
| 4 | 넷 | net |
| 5 | 다섯 | daseot |
| 6 | 여섯 | yeoseot |
| 7 | 일곱 | ilgop |
| 8 | 여덟 | yeodeol |
| 9 | 아홉 | ahop |
| 10 | 열 | yeol |
| 11 | 열하나 | yeolhana |

> Native Korean numbers are used for: counting items (개), people (명), books (권), cups (잔), and hours (시).

CHAPTER - 안부 [anbu] (Saying Hello)

Learning Goals: Greetings & Talking about recent events

## TLDR - Key Points
Essential Grammar:
- -고 [go] → "and" (connects actions: 공부하고 여행했어요 [gongbuhago yeohaenghaesseoyo])
- 그런데 [geureonde] → "but/however" (shows contrast: 바빠요. 그런데 시간 있어요 [bappayo. geureonde sigan isseoyo])
- 도 [do] → "also/too" (adds emphasis: 친구도 만나고 [chingudo mannago])

## Dialogues

Dialogue 1 - Catching up after vacation:
치엔: 수진 씨, 오랜만이에요. 그동안 잘 지냈어요? [chien: sujin ssi, oraenman-ieyo. geudongan jal jinaesseoyo?]
     → Chien: Sujin, it's been so long. How have you been?
수진: 네, 잘 지냈어요. 치엔 씨는 휴가 때 뭐 했어요? [sujin: ne, jal jinaesseoyo. chien ssineun hyuga ttae mwo haesseoyo?]
     → Sujin: Yes, I've been well. What did you do during vacation?
치엔: 저는 한국어 공부도 하고 여행도 했어요. 수진 씨는요? [chien: jeoneun hangugeo gongbudo hago yeohaengdo haesseoyo. sujin ssineunyo?]
     → Chien: I studied Korean and traveled. What about you?
수진: 저는 집에서 많이 쉬었어요. [sujin: jeoneun jibeseo mani swieosseoyo]
     → Sujin: I rested at home a lot.

Dialogue 2 - Asking about current situation:
민수: 투이 씨, 요즘 어떻게 지내요? [minsu: tui ssi, yojeum eotteoke jinaeyo?]
     → Minsoo: Tui, how are you doing these days?
투이: 요즘은 좀 바빠요. 그래서 정신이 없어요. [tui: yojeumeun jom bappayo. geuraeseo jeongsini eopseoyo]
     → Tui: I'm a bit busy these days. So it's hectic.
민수: 민수 씨는요? [tui: minsu ssineunyo?]
     → Tui: What about you, Minsoo?
민수: 저는 그저 그래요. 학교도 다니고 아르바이트도 해요. [minsu: jeoneun geujeo geuraeyo. hakgyodo danigo areubaiteudo haeyo]
     → Minsoo: Nothing special. I go to school and work part-time.
     그런데 저는 이번 주에 시험이 하나 있어요. 그래서 조금 힘들어요. [geureonde jeoneun ibeon jue siheomi hana isseoyo. geuraeseo jogeum himdeureoyo]
     → But I have a test this week. So it's a bit tough.

## Vocabulary

Greetings & Status (안부 anbu):
- 오랜만이에요 [oraenman-ieyo] → It's been so long
- 그동안 잘 지냈어요? [geudongan jal jinaesseoyo?] → How have you been?
- 방학을 잘 보냈어요? [banghageul jal bonaesseoyo?] → Did you have a good vacation?
- 휴가 때 뭐 했어요? [hyuga ttae mwo haesseoyo?] → What did you do during vacation?
- 요즘 어떻게 지내요? [yojeum eotteoke jinaeyo?] → How are you doing these days?
- 잘 지내요 [jal jinaeyo] → quite well

Current State - Recent Events (근황 geunhwang):
- 바쁘다 [bappeuda] → to be busy
- 힘들다 [himdeulda] → to have a hard time
- 피곤하다 [pigonhada] → to be tired
- 정신이 없다 [jeongsini eopda] → hectic, in a daze
- 그저 그렇다 [geujeo geureota] → nothing special, so-so
- 재미있다 [jaemi-itda] → fun, interesting
- 재미없다 [jaemi-eopda] → not fun, boring
- 즐겁다 [jeulgeopda] → to be pleased, enjoyable

Activities (things you do in daily life):
- 휴가 [hyuga] → vacation | 방학 [banghak] → school vacation
- 여행 [yeohaeng] → trip | 출장 [chulhang] → business trip
- 공부 [gongbu] → study | 아르바이트 [areubaiteu] → part-time job  
- 시험 [siheom] → test | 쉬다 [swida] → to rest
- 청소하다 [cheongsohada] → to clean | 책을 읽다 [chaekeul ikda] → to read books
- 친구를 만나다 [chingureul mannada] → to meet friends

Time & Frequency:
- 요즘 [yojeum] → these days | 그동안 [geudongan] → meanwhile, during that time
- 많이 [mani] → a lot | 늦다 [neutda] → to be late
- 보냈어요 [bonaesseoyo] → spent/passed (time)

Weather & Description:
- 덥고 [deopgo] → hot and | 춥고 [chupgo] → cold and
- 따뜻하다 [ttatteuthada] → to be warm | 시원하다 [siwonhada] → to be cool
- 비가 와요 [biga wayo] → it's raining | 눈이 와요 [nuni wayo] → it's snowing
- 달라요 [dallayo] → different

Others:
- 인사 [insa] → greetings/regards | 안부 [anbu] → regards/well-being
- 음식 [eumsik] → food | 음악 [eumak] → music
- 바다 [bada] → beach/sea | 자전거 [jajeongeo] → bicycle

## Grammar Points

**1. -고 (and)** | Formation: Verb stem + 고
Usage: Connects two actions/events equally (equivalent to "and" in English)
Examples:
- 한국어 공부도 하고 여행도 했어요 [gongbudo hago yeohaengdo haesseoyo] 
  → I studied Korean and traveled
- 학교도 다니고 아르바이트도 해요 [hakgyodo danigo areubaiteudo haeyo] 
  → I go to school and work part-time
- 오늘은 덥고 비가 와요 [oneureun deopgo biga wayo] 
  → Today it's hot and raining

**2. 그런데 [geureonde] (but, however, by the way)**
Usage: Shows contrast between statements or introduces a new topic
Examples:
- 저는 그저 그래요. 그런데 이번 주에 시험이 있어요 [jeoneun geujeo geuraeyo. geureonde ibeon jue siheomi isseoyo]
  → I'm doing okay. But I have a test this week
- 이번 주에는 바빠요. 그런데 다음 주에는 시간이 있어요 [ibeon jueneun bappayo. geureonde daeum jueneun sigani isseoyo]
  → I'm busy this week. But I'll have time next week

**3. 도 [do] (also, too)**
Usage: Attached to nouns to mean "also" or "too"
Examples:
- 친구도 만나고 [chingudo mannago] → also meet friends
- 한국어 공부도 하고 [hangugeo gongbudo hago] → also study Korean

**4. 그리고 [geurigo] vs 그런데 [geureonde]**
- 그리고 = "and" (adds information)
- 그런데 = "but/however" (shows contrast)

## Key Patterns & Useful Phrases

Starting Conversations (when meeting someone after a while):
- 오랜만이에요! [oraenman-ieyo!] → It's been so long!
- 그동안 잘 지냈어요? [geudongan jal jinaesseoyo?] → How have you been?
- 요즘 어떻게 지내요? [yojeum eotteoke jinaeyo?] → How are you doing these days?

Follow-up Questions (to continue the conversation):
- [Name] 씨는요? [[Name] ssineunyo?] → What about you, [Name]?
- 휴가/방학 때 뭐 했어요? [hyuga/banghak ttae mwo haesseoyo?] → What did you do during vacation?

Common Responses (how to answer about your situation):
- 네, 잘 지냈어요 [ne, jal jinaesseoyo] → Yes, I've been well
- 그저 그래요 [geujeo geuraeyo] → Nothing special  
- 정신이 없어요 [jeongsini eopseoyo] → It's hectic
- 요즘은 좀 바빠요 [yojeumeun jom bappayo] → I'm a bit busy these days

## Additional Class Notes & Expressions

- 안 갔어요 [an gasseoyo] → didn't go (by choice)
- 못 갔어요 [mot gasseoyo] → couldn't go (unable to)
- 청소 안 했어요 [cheongso an haesseoyo] → didn't clean
- 수업이 즐거워요. 그래서 기뻐요 [sueobi jeulgeowoyo. geuraeseo gippeoyo] → Class is enjoyable. So I'm happy
- 좋아요 [joahayo] → is good (describing something) | 좋아해요 [joahaeyo] → I like (personal preference)
- 일이 많아요. 그래서 정신이 없어요 [iri manayo. geuraeseo jeongsini eopseoyo] → There's a lot of work. So I'm busy/out of my mind
- 고르세요 [goreuseyo] → select/choose
- 오늘은 춥고 눈이 와요 [oneureun chupgo nuni wayo] → Today it's cold and snowing
- 오늘은 덥고 비가 와요 [oneureun deopgo biga wayo] → Today it's hot and raining
- 저는 아이스크림을 좋아해요 [jeoneun aiseukrimeul joahaeyo] → I like ice cream
- 아이스크림은 건강에 좋지 않아요 [aiseukrimeun geongang-e jochi anhayo] → Ice cream is not good for health
